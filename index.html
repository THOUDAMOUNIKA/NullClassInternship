Machine Learning Projects

Eye-Color Detection

Eye color detection is the process of identifying the color of the iris within an image of a person's face.It typically involves computer vision techniques to analyze the image and extract features related to the iris.The detected eye color can provide valuable information in various applications such as biometric identification systems.

Baldness Detection

Baldness detection involves analyzing images of individual scalps to determine the extent of hair loss or bald patches. This process often employs computer vision algorithms to identify regions of the scalp with reduced hair density or complete baldness. It serves as a valuable tool in cosmetic surgery, dermatology, and personalized healthcare for assessing hair loss.

Detect Child or Not

Detecting whether an individual is a child or not typically involves analyzing facial features,such as the size and shape of the face, eyes, and nose. Machine learning models trained on age-related datasets can classify images based on these features to determine if the person appears to be a child or an adult.This technology has applications in age verification for online services, child protection systems, and age-sensitive content filtering.

Voice Gender Detection

Voice gender detection is the process of determining whether a given audio sample corresponds to a male or female speaker based on acoustic features, commonly applied in voice-related applications and services.
[NOTE] Use the audio files in ".wav" format only

Pedestrian Detection

Pedestrian detection is a computer vision task that involves identifying and locating pedestrians within images or video frames using various algorithms and techniques, often employed for applications in autonomous vehicles, surveillance, and pedestrian safety.
